## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge changes in their area of ownership
- Triage issues and discussions
- Keep governance and security files current
- Uphold the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Coordinate releases or publication workflows as relevant for the repository type

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have made significant and sustained contributions may be nominated by an existing maintainer. See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
